Every estimate and reference photo is saved locally on your device. KoreFlow does not upload your data to our servers. Uninstalling the app will permanently remove all of your saved quotes — please export anything you need first.
KoreFlow's pricing model is built on transparent, observable inputs: property type, room count, floor access, distance, add-ons, and speed tier. Estimates are designed to be a planning aid — not a binding quote. For final pricing on a real engagement, always request an on-site assessment from a licensed service provider.

GET THE MOST OUT OF KOREFLOW

Tips for sharper estimates

Three small habits that materially improve the accuracy of your quotes.

1

Measure twice

Double-check your room count and approximate area. The estimator scales labor with these inputs, so small errors compound.

2

Attach reference photos

A picture of staircases, narrow doorways, or bulky items lets your future service provider prepare properly on day one.

3

Try multiple speed tiers

Same-Day and Priority tiers are useful comparisons even if you plan to book Standard — they reveal how time-sensitive the work is.
