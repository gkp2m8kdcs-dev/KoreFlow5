Where your data lives

KoreFlow saves every estimate and reference photo directly on this device. Estimates are kept in app-local storage and your attached photos are written to the app's private documents folder.

Nothing is uploaded to our servers by the app. We do not maintain a cloud copy of your data, and we do not sync your estimates between devices. The app works fully offline once installed.

Permissions you may be asked for

KoreFlow requests only the permissions it needs to perform a feature you actively use. Each is prompted at the moment of use — never at launch.

  • Photos — to attach reference pictures of rooms, furniture, or fragile items to an estimate, and to save your finalized quote summary as an image to your library.
  • Camera — to capture a new reference photo from inside the app when you do not already have a suitable picture.
  • Location (while in use) — used only on-screen to estimate pickup-to-destination distance for moving and freight quotes. KoreFlow does not request background location.
  • Contacts — optional, and only when you tap "Import from Contacts" to pre-fill an address. KoreFlow never reads your contact list in the background.

How each permission is used

Each permission is requested at the moment you need it. You can deny any of them and the rest of the app still works.

For example, denying location access does not lock the app — you can simply type the pickup and destination addresses and the distance field becomes a manual entry. Denying photo access prevents you from attaching reference images, but the estimator itself remains fully usable.

Third parties and tracking

KoreFlow does not include advertising SDKs, analytics trackers, or crash-reporting services. We do not sell, share, or resell your data.

The "Contact Support" button opens the system Mail composer (governed by Apple's own privacy rules) and the "Visit Website" / "Help Center" buttons open Safari via SFSafariViewController. These are system-provided surfaces and inherit their own privacy posture.

Saved quotes and "Clear All"

Saved quotes and any attached photos can be removed at any time from the Profile screen. Tapping Clear All Saved Quotes permanently deletes the JSON-encoded estimate list from app-local storage and removes the corresponding photo files inside the app's private folder.

You can also remove a single estimate at any time from the Saved Quotes screen. There is no soft-delete or recovery window — once cleared, the data is gone.

Account, backup, and sync

There is no KoreFlow account system. Estimates are not backed up to iCloud by the app and they do not sync between devices. Uninstalling the app removes everything you saved, including any in-app data and reference photos.

If you have an iCloud or device backup enabled at the system level, certain app data may be included in those backups according to Apple's defaults. This is controlled in the iOS Settings app, not by KoreFlow.

Children's privacy

KoreFlow is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact Amirjadoon8811@gmail.com so we can delete it.

Changes to this policy

If we update how KoreFlow handles your data, we will revise this page inside the app and on this website. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

Because KoreFlow does not collect identifying information about you, we cannot notify you personally of changes. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
